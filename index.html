Data Deletion and Privacy Choices

Last updated: July 11, 2025

Your Privacy Rights

FocusTodo respects your privacy and gives you complete control over your personal data. You can request data deletion at any time.

How to Delete Your Account and Data

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open FocusTodo app
  2. Go to the Settings tab
  3. Tap "Sign Out"
  4. Your local data will be cleared immediately

Option 2: Complete Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account and all data from our servers:

  1. Send an email to our privacy team
  2. Include "Delete My Account" in the subject line
  3. We will process your request within 48 hours
  4. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete

What Data Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently remove:

  • Your email address and authentication credentials
  • All your tasks and task history
  • Account creation and modification timestamps
  • Any other personal information associated with your account

Data Retention

After deletion:

  • Your data is permanently removed from our active servers
  • Backups are purged within 30 days
  • No data is retained for marketing or analytics purposes

Export Your Data

Before deleting your account, you may want to export your tasks. Currently, you can manually copy your tasks from the app.

Contact Us

Email: k.s.11626@ezweb.ne.jp

Response Time: Within 48 hours

Please include your account email address in your request.
